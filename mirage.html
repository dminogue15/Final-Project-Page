Mirage is an agile, wind-based Hero with powerful abilities and disruption. By throwing out Fire Scarabs and transforming into a Tornado, Mirage can damage, lift up, and steal life from his targets while applying Djinn's Mark to enemies to enhance his attacks. When the time is right, he can Travel across the map in an instant to assist his allies in escaping or hunting down weak foes.
An emissary of the Djinn ambassador Nashala Dion, Mirage is a diplomatic bodyguard who is charged with carrying and protecting the djinn's vessel.
